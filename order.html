    Question:How does Zappify 2.0 work?

    Zappify 2.0 uses LEDs to attract mosquitoes, and then utilizes an electric coil which kills those mosquitoes, dead!

    Question:Does Zappify 2.0 require a wall outlet for power?

    No! Zappify 2.0 is powered by a 2000mah Lithium rechargeable battery that provides up to 13 hours of use per charge. Just plug Zappify 2.0 into any powered USB port when you need to recharge it.

    Question:I'm not good with gadgets. How easy is Zappify 2.0 to use?

    It’s incredibly easy to use Zappify 2.0! Simply take it out of the packaging, charge it via the included USB cord, and you’re ready to go! Set it upright or hang it nearly anywhere. Cleaning is as easy as using the included brush to sweep mosquitoes and debris from the electric coil for disposal.

    Question:What are some tips to get the most out of my Zappify 2.0?

    Tip #1. For best results, place Zappify 2.0 in the area you wish to use and leave it operating for at least two hours. This will rid the area of all mosquitoes beforehand.


    Tip #2. Because the device relies on the LEDs to lure mosquitoes, minimizing ambient light in the background will give better results. Having too many lights on will be distracting and you may not see the results you expect.

    Question:Is Zappify 2.0 safe to use around children and pets?

    Zappify 2.0 kills mosquitoes without the use of chemicals or pesticides and is completely safe to use around children or pets.

    Question:Are mosquitoes really something we should be worried about?

    Sadly, yes! Each year, mosquitos are responsible for more human deaths than any other animal. They can carry and transmit terrible diseases such as West Nile virus, Zika virus, encephalitis, malaria, and many others.

    Question:What is the area coverage?

    Zappify 2.0 emits a 360° zap zone to create a mosquito-free area up to 30 square meters (322 square feet).

    Question:Can it be used both indoors and outdoors?

    Yes, absolutely!

    Question:What is the voltage rating?

    Grid features 2000 volts of zapping power but it is harmless to kids and animals.
